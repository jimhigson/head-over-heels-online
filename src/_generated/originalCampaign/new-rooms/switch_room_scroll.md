## Notice to All Minions

![](texture-blacktooth_wall_shield_away?bg-pureBlack)![](texture-animated-bubbles_cold?float-right)By Imperial Decree, this chamber has been fitted with an Appearance Modulation Device.

*Step on the button to cycle the castle's visual rendering*. The Emperor grew tired of the monochrome decor after thirty-eight years and commissioned a colourised renovation from some enthusiast on the internet, who for reasons best known to himself spent the last year painstakingly remaking our castle.

The second setting restores the original monochrome look, for those minions who prefer squinting at things the old-fashioned way.

The third style was contributed by a talented artist called Toppy, who painstakingly redrew every sprite from scratch in his own distinctive vision. The Architect considers it rather good.

Observant minions will note that Toppy has not yet got round to redecorating the walls and floors.

The switch on the far wall activates a Cathode Ray Tube simulation. The Architect insists the castle used to be viewed through one of these devices. It hides the individual pixels and bends all the straight edges, which he considers an improvement.

Do not adjust either device during crown inspections.

*> For further visual adjustments, consult the Settings menu. The Architect has hidden additional options there that he couldn't fit on a single button.*
